# SPEC.md — Project Specification

> **Status**: `DRAFTING`

## Vision
To create a buttery-smooth, high-performance portfolio website with a "crazy" new landing page that deeply engages visitors without compromising on load times or scrolling performance. 

## Goals
1. Identify and fix existing performance bottlenecks to ensure a smooth scrolling and rendering experience.
2. Design and implement a visually stunning and highly engaging landing page. 

## Non-Goals (Out of Scope)
- Complete rewrite of the backend infrastructure (unless absolutely necessary for performance).
- Changes to unrelated pages (focus remains on the landing page and global performance).

## Users
Recruiters, clients, and other developers looking at the portfolio.

## Constraints
- Must maintain a 60FPS scroll performance.
- Fast initial load times.

## Success Criteria
- [ ] No visible stuttering or dropping frames during scroll anywhere on the site.
- [ ] The landing page provides a distinct "wow" factor upon load.
